## Session Summary

From proof-of-concept to production, the last 20 percent of GenAI adoption is where real work and real value happen.

This session focuses on what comes after the demo, when hallucinations, integration friction, and missing context collide with live engineering systems.

## Key Themes

- Embedding GenAI in sociotechnical systems
- Hands-on customization and prompt playbooks
- Team enablement through immersive learning
- Integrating AI into platforms such as MCP, GitHub, and Kubernetes
- Shaping culture, architecture, and feedback loops for durable adoption

## Session Abstract

Expect war stories, not wonderland. This talk is for teams serious about making GenAI stick in enterprise environments without disrupting delivery flow.
